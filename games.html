How Cognitive Training Works

The science of cognitive improvement in 4 steps

1

Challenge

Task demands exceed current ability activating the brain's stress response and signaling for adaptation

2

Strain

Neural circuits fire intensely under cognitive load synaptic connections strengthen through long-term potentiation

3

Adapt

The brain rewires: axons myelinate, dendrites branch, and the targeted network becomes structurally more efficient

4

Transfer

Improvements in trained tasks generalize to untrained real-world tasks using the same neural circuits
