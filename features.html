📚 AI Book Retention

Read More. Remember More.

Reading alone builds vocabulary and knowledge but most information is forgotten within 48 hours without active retrieval practice. LockedIn's AI-generated retention quizzes transform passive reading into deep encoding.

Roediger & Karpicke (2006) demonstrated that testing yourself on material produces 50–70% better long-term retention than re-reading an effect called the "testing effect." LockedIn's AI automatically generates quiz questions for any book, applying this principle at scale.

  • ✓ Growing digital library organized by level (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)
  • ✓ Text and audio reading modes
  • ✓ AI-generated retention tests after completing each book
  • ✓ Book progress tracking and reading history
  • ✓ Search and filter by title, author, category, and difficulty level
  • ✓ Retention scores feed into the overall Focus Score

🩺 Diagnostic Assessment

Know Your Cognitive Baseline

You cannot improve what you cannot measure. LockedIn's diagnostic assessment uses four validated clinical and research scales to establish your cognitive and behavioral baseline across the dimensions most relevant to focus and productivity.

Unlike self-report tools from wellness apps, each dimension maps directly to a published psychometric instrument used in clinical and academic research giving your scores genuine scientific grounding.

  • ✓ Screen Habits based on Smartphone Addiction Scale-Short Version (SAS-SV, Kwon et al., 2013)
  • ✓ Attention based on ASRS-v1.1 Adult ADHD Self-Report Scale (Kessler et al., 2005 / WHO)
  • ✓ Lifestyle based on Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index (PSQI) + WHO Physical Activity Guidelines
  • ✓ Learning based on Need for Cognition Scale (NCS, Cacioppo & Petty, 1982)
  • ✓ Results inform personalized coaching suggestions and game prioritization
